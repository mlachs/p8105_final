There are many activities available to you when visiting a national park. Between all the parks there are 99 unique activities. Ranging from ATV Off-Roading to Wildlife Watching. This range of activites bring millions of people to our national parks each year. This got us thinking, do the number of activites avalible at each park affect thhe level of visitation? We did some data analysis to find out.
